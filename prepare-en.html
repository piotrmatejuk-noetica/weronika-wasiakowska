Important

Safety before method

Hypnotherapy does not replace medical or psychiatric diagnosis and treatment, or psychotherapy. Before the session, please tell Weronika about:

  • a current mental health crisis or suicidal thoughts,
  • a diagnosis of psychosis, mania, epilepsy or a significant neurological condition,
  • medication or other circumstances that may affect attention or awareness.

This does not automatically rule out working together. It allows an informed assessment of whether this approach is appropriate and whether consultation with your treating clinician is advisable.
